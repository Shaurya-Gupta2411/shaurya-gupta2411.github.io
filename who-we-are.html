WHO WE ARE

INTRODUCTION

We are an Engineering Consultancy Company which specialises in Project and Programme Management, Engineering and Design Management, and Infrastructure & Transportation Advisory Services for our clients in a safe, sustainable and innovative manner.

We provide professional services in Project Management, Design Management, Due Diligence, Independent Audits, Project Reviews, Dispute Resolution for Highways, Railway, Trams & Metros, Water, Airports, Bridges, and Transportation Planning projects.

We have an international purpose, providing services from our headquarters in London to many countries. We have experience in Europe, Middle East, LATAM, North Africa, Asia and Asia Pacific.
