Bangalore New International Airport – Airport Development

Our team supported BIAL Bangalore International Airport in the first phase, to review the work undertaken by Architect KMP.  This technical support focused on the architectural and engineering services for the terminal building, all other airport related buildings, the airside and landside facilities including infrastructure. In a second phase, we provided design review services during design, construction and commissioning phase. The entire project design and construction cost was approx. € 290m.
